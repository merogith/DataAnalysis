Key findings

  • The economy runs a mild inflation: coin sinks recover only ~63% of coins minted, and 83% of players' wallets keep growing.
  • Two premium items — Phoenix Blade (61% win rate) and Aegis Shield (59%) — are statistically overpowered (CIs entirely above 50%), pointing to pay-to-win.
  • Retention shows a textbook Day-1 cliff: 47% return on day 1, falling to 18% by day 7 and 4% by day 30.
  • A logistic-regression model predicts which players become payers from their first-3-day behaviour with AUC 0.80 (payer rate 5.8%, ARPPU $15.61).
Recommendation: Add coin sinks to curb inflation, rebalance the two overpowered premium items, and invest in the Day-1 onboarding experience to flatten the retention cliff.
