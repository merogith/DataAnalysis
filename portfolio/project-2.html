Key findings

  • The economy runs a mild inflation: coin sinks recover only about 63% of the coins minted, and most players' wallets keep growing.
  • Two premium items, Phoenix Blade (61% win rate) and Aegis Shield (59%), are statistically overpowered, with confidence intervals entirely above 50%.
  • Retention falls sharply after the first day: 47% return on day 1, 18% by day 7, and 4% by day 30.
  • A logistic-regression model predicts which players become payers from their first three days of behaviour, with an AUC of 0.80 (payer rate 5.8%, ARPPU $15.61).
Recommendation: Add coin sinks to curb the inflation, rebalance the two overpowered premium items, and invest in the first-day experience to slow the early drop in retention.
