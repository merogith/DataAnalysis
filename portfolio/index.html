US Stock Market: Trends, Risk and Valuation

Where does the market stand, how risky has it been, and is it expensive today?
Built on more than 150 years of real market data. Valuation has tracked future returns: a higher CAPE has been followed by lower 10-year returns (p ≈ 1e-40), and today's reading is in the top 5% of all history. Worst real drawdown on record: −81%; gold's correlation with stocks is about 0.
Tech: Python · pandas · statsmodels (OLS) · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite window functions · real Shiller / CBOE / LBMA data

Pharmaceutical CRM Sales Analytics

Do sales calls drive prescriptions, and which doctors should the team prioritise?
Calls and prescriptions are related (r = 0.49, p = 1.5e-37), with each extra call per year worth about 5.5 more scripts. A KMeans segmentation splits 600 doctors into four groups and isolates a 99-doctor high-potential list to focus on.
Tech: Python · pandas · scikit-learn (KMeans) · statsmodels · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite RANK() OVER

Free-to-Play Game Economy and Balance

Is the economy balanced, are any items overpowered, and what keeps players spending?
The economy runs a mild inflation (coin sinks recover only 63% of what is minted), two premium items are statistically overpowered (Phoenix Blade at a 61% win rate), retention drops from 47% to 18% to 4% across D1/D7/D30, and a logistic model predicts paying players with an AUC of 0.80.
Tech: Python · pandas · scikit-learn (logistic regression) · proportion z-tests · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite window functions
