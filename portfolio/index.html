US Stock Market: Trends, Risk & Valuation

“150 years of real market data — where are we, how risky is it, and what should we expect next?”
Key result: market valuation (CAPE) statistically predicts the next decade's returns (p ≈ 1e-40); today's valuation is in the top 5% of all history. Worst-ever real drawdown: −81%; gold's correlation with stocks ≈ 0.
Tech: Python · pandas · statsmodels (OLS) · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite window functions · real Shiller / CBOE / LBMA data

Pharmaceutical CRM Sales Analytics

“Which doctors should the sales team prioritise, and do sales calls actually drive prescriptions?”
Key result: calls correlate with prescriptions (r = 0.49, p = 1.5e-37); each extra call/year ≈ 5.5 more scripts. KMeans grouped 600 doctors into 4 segments and surfaced a 99-doctor target list.
Tech: Python · pandas · scikit-learn (KMeans) · statsmodels · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite RANK() OVER

Free-to-Play Game Economy & Balance

“Is the economy balanced, which items are overpowered, and what makes players stay and pay?”
Key result: the economy runs a mild inflation (sinks recover only 63% of minted coins); Phoenix Blade (61% win rate) is overpowered & pay-to-win; retention shows a Day-1 cliff (47% → 18% → 4%); payer model AUC 0.80.
Tech: Python · pandas · scikit-learn (logistic regression) · proportion z-tests · matplotlib/seaborn · SQLite window functions
