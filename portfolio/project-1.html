Key findings

  • Sales calls and prescriptions are positively related (r = 0.49, p = 1.5e-37); each extra call per year is worth about 5.5 more scripts.
  • Prescriptions are highly concentrated — the top 20% of doctors drive ~37% of all volume.
  • KMeans clustering split 600 doctors into 4 segments and surfaced a 99-doctor 'high-potential growth' group with high potential but below-peak volume.
  • Top product was Cardiplex and top region the Southeast; the rep leaderboard shows a wide performance gap to coach.
Recommendation: Re-allocate call effort toward the 99 high-potential doctors and coach the lowest-performing reps using the top reps' playbook.
