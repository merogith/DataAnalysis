Key findings

  • Sales calls and prescriptions are positively related (r = 0.49, p = 1.5e-37). Each extra call per year is worth about 5.5 more scripts.
  • Prescriptions are concentrated: the top 20% of doctors account for roughly 37% of all volume.
  • A KMeans segmentation split 600 doctors into four groups and isolated a 99-doctor high-potential group with high potential but below-peak volume.
  • The top product was Cardiplex and the top region the Southeast, and the rep leaderboard shows a wide performance gap.
Recommendation: Reallocate call effort toward the 99 high-potential doctors, and coach the lowest-performing reps using the methods of the top performers.
